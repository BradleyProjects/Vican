What Is a Google My Business Listing?

Google My Business listing is an online tool for businesses and organisations to manage their online presence across Google, including Search and Maps.


Why should your business have an Google My Business profile? To help customers find your business, and to tell them your story, you can verify your business and edit your business information. 


Did you know, that if you appear in the 3 pack of Google my business you will be the second set of results on page one of Google search? If there is no ads running in your specific niche you will be on top of page one of Google search. How do you get into the 3 pack you might ask, with setting up your profile correctly, with the perfect keywords and with customer reviews, we can help you achieve this call us today!

The Benefit Of Us Setting Up Your Profile:

  • Be discoverable

    Search is digital. Even if you run an old fashioned brick and mortar shop, your customers and prospects are online. And that’s where they’ll typically start their search for your company, products, or services. Whether you’re looking for foot traffic or web traffic, Google is the ultimate search referrer. A Google My Business account ensures that when someone looks your company up on Google Search and Google Maps, they find it. Once they do, your listing shows searches where and how to visit your shop, whether you have a web or physical address.

  • Keep customers informed

    Your Google My Business listing includes contact information, business hours, services, products and other essential details. You can post updates to share that you’ve expanded services, temporarily closed, or fully reopened (an especially useful feature during emergency situations like COVID-19). Google business accounts have strong local SEO, so the information you share will rank above other sites.

  • Boost consumer confidence

    A Google My Business profile lends credibility to your company. Here are some stats on researches done. Customers are 70% more likely to visit businesses with a Google My Business listing. Another study found that businesses with complete listings on Google are twice as likely to earn trust from customers.

Let The Customers Find You Instead Of Your Competition!

Have you heard about Waze and Apple Maps? Let us set-up these two features for you and be one step ahead of your competitor.

WAZE

Waze is a community-based traffic and navigation app. It uses GPS and user feedback to provide and real time traffic updates. Similar like Google My business which show your business location on google maps, you can also add your place or business location to the Waze app, so that when people searching they can get proper directions. 


Creating an online presence is important in this digital era regarding any business category. Adding your business location to Waze may help to attract and navigate your customer to your location, which is a good thing for your business.

APPLE MAPS

The continued growth and development of Apple Maps — along with the introduction of Apple Maps ratings and user-submitted photos — is bound to boost the importance of getting your business listed on the platform. Tapping into this can put your business in front of customers at critical points as they make shopping decisions. 


With ratings and reviews being a major local SEO ranking factor, the way your business appears on Apple Maps can also have a major impact on your online reputation. It is certainly worthwhile to add your business to Apple Maps, at the very least, and to prepare to adapt to the navigation app’s upcoming changes and updates.
