Debt Counselling Process

Our Debt Counselling Process that makes a difference:

Step 1

Analyse the financial situation of the client

Step 2

All documents required for application are gathered for a formal review

Step 3

New proposals sent to creditors of confirmation for new restructured payment plan

Step 4

Client is listed on the NCR Debt Help System and at the Credit Bureaus as being under debt review to enjoy relief from legal action

Step 5

Once all creditors confirm restructured payment plan, Consent Order is granted and monthly payments can commence

Step 6

During the process, when smaller debts get paid in full, these monthly distributions will be used towards larger debts ensuring that your debt is paid as quickly as possible

Step 7

Once all accounts are settled in full (except for your home loan, if applicable), you can exit the debt review process by obtaining a Form 19 Clearance Certificate which will ensure that your status is updated on the NCR Debt Help System and the debt review indicator is removed at the Credit Bureaus leaving you financially free

Step 8

Under certain circumstances, you may choose to withdraw from the debt review process by bringing an Application to Court for Rescission of the Debt Review. In order to do this, however, we will need to show that you are no longer over-indebted
