Who should join?

If any of the following describe you, then M3 is likely a good fit:

  • People who are actively running the marketing for businesses generating at least 7-figures (or entrepreneurs who own at least a 6-figure business).
  • People who have been doing marketing for a minimum of 5 years.
  • People who want accountability to hit their goals (and the support to get them there).
  • People who know their business can grow, and who are willing to work to make that happen.
  • People who want to avoid the painful “learning curves” and avoid mistakes as they grow.
  • People who are already successful, and want to double their income & their time off.
When does the program start?

The M3 experience includes a customized onboarding and kickstart, so you can get started right away.

What’s the time commitment?

You and your team can attend as much or as little of the weekly “drop-in” coaching calls and special interest group mini-masterminds as you like. All sessions are recorded and questions can be submitted ahead of time if you can’t make a session.

That said, we do ask that you commit about 2-days per quarter for the mastermind meetings.

Beyond that, everything else is as-needed and when-needed

Do I get 1:1 support and coaching?

M3 members get access to weekly “drop-in” coaching calls with our mentors. No-holds-barred: ask industry veterans anything and get a direct, specific answer you can use.

Members also get up to 5 expert campaign reviews per year! Our premier Certified Partners will review your campaigns and provide strategic feedback. You’ll leave with a report of findings and actionable steps.

Are there live events or things I will need to travel for?

Up to you! We hold 3 in-person mastermind meetings a year in Austin, TX plus a special pre-event at Traffic & Conversion Summit in San Diego. You can choose to either join us in-person or live stream the meetings from your home or office.

When are the live events?

The live, in-person M3 meetings are typically held in January, May, December, and the annual Traffic & Conversion Summit in September. Exact dates, meeting locations, and a complete calendar of events will be shared during the onboarding call.

Where are the live events?

Our live meetings are typically held in Austin, Texas and San Diego, California.

What if I’m based outside the US?

We actively accept members from all over the world, however, the majority of sessions will be in North American time zones and the in-person events take place in Austin, TX. All recordings are made available plus the group is active and available 24/7 for support.

My business is still pre-revenue and I’m just getting started, is this right for me?

No, not yet. M3 is for experienced marketers and entrepreneurs running established businesses with 6 and 7-figure revenue momentum.

Show up, contribute, gain massive momentum, and do the work to create lasting success for yourself and your business.
