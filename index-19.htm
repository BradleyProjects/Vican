Expertly Designed Synthetic Thatch Roof

Fiber Thatch is a local company based in the beautiful country of South Africa! Our company was founded in 1976 by our chairman Jan Grobler. We are the global leading manufacturer and exporter of synthetic thatch products and tiles. We have agents and distributors based in every country in the world.


At Fiber Thatch we pride ourselves on not only offering a permanent roofing solution but, also a product with all the benefits that looks like the real thing. We offer one of the largest colour ranges of synthetic thatch tiles available in a reed or palm finish.

About Us

Crafted To Perfection

Fiber Thatch boasts synthetic roofing that has been crafted using the finest plastics. This ensures that the quality of the thatch products is high.

 

Fiber Thatch Roofs are also environmentally friendly material being used in the process of making the thatch roofing.


With many styles to choose from we encourage you to let your imagination run wild so your area can take on the unique aesthetic you deserve.

Our Synthetic Thatch Products

  • Fiber Reed


    Fiber Reed is our synthetic thatch option that emulate & imitate traditional reed style thatch. This style of thatch is popular in Northern Europe, United Kingdom and the Southern African regions.


    Learn More about Fiber Reed

  • Fiber Palm


    Fiber Palm is our synthetic thatch option that emulate & imitate thinner leaf Palm Thatch. This style of thatch is popular for the roofing of resorts and hotel complexes and is extremely economical, durable and realistic.


    Learn More about Fiber Palm

  • FR - MO Boards


    The FR-MO Boards has been designed according to specifications provided by the Fiber Roofing team to be used as sub roof material with the Fiber Roofing range of Fiber Synthetic Tiles.


    Learn More about FR - MO Boards

  • Fiber Poles


    Fiber Roofing cc has through the past five decades become one of the biggest suppliers of CCA treated Eucalyptus Grandis poles, droppers and laths. 


    Learn More about Fiber Poles

  • Fiber Interiors


    To Complement Our Fiber Synthetic Reed And Palm, We Have Developed 4 Different Types Of Ceilings To Be Used On The Interior Roofs.



    Learn More about Fiber Interiors

  • Custom Colours


    We can manufacture our synthetic thatch in any colour you desire, we could match a prescribed colour brief 100%.



    Learn More about Custom Colours
