FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTionS

How Will I Make Money As A Certified Partner?

There are multiple ways you can generate additional revenue as a Certified Partner. First, you can go back to the clients you already have and offer them one of the many turnkey products and services you’ll be licensed to sell and deliver. This is the low-hanging fruit, and depending on your existing client base could easily generate tens of thousands of dollars in revenue. You can also utilize the tools and resources we will provide you (including, but not limited to the “73-Point Business Doubling Checklist,” the pass-along doodle video as well as your newfound certification and branding) to attract new clients…even clients who may have passed on your services in the past.

What Companies Are A Good Fit For The Program?

The ideal Certified Partner should be an agency or consultancy that is actively serving small businesses. We don’t care if it’s hundreds of small businesses or just a handful…if you love marketing and you’re passionate about helping businesses grow and succeed, then we want you on our team! You could be a business coach, SEO agency, web design firm…it really doesn’t matter! If you serve small businesses and “digital marketing” touches some aspect of your deliverable, then there’s a good chance you’re a fit.

What Level Of Access Will I Have To The DigitalMarketer Team?

You will be directly supported by our designated Partner Success Team who is available to you for any and all questions within the CP Community, by email, and regularly scheduled Zoom sessions. This team also has access to anyone else who may need to be brought into the loop. So in a very real sense, you’ll have access to anyone on the team who can help make your partnership with us a success.

How Many Partners Do You Allow Into The Program?

We haven’t set a limit at this time. Our main goal is to help as many small businesses as possible, and given that there are literally tens of millions of small businesses in the U.S. alone, we need all the help we can get.

Do You Work With Partners Outside The U.S.?

Absolutely! Our goal is to take DigitalMarketer international. We want DM offices in every major city in every country that will have us, but we know to do that we need “boots on the ground” who are familiar with the territory. That said, right now all the DigitalMarketer materials are in English only, so if your market requires a different language you will need to take on the translation work.

How Will The Investment Yield A Positive ROI For Me Or My Company?

Every business is different so it’s impossible to guarantee a specific revenue or ROI result. That said, we are aligned with the same goal of helping small businesses grow, so we will do everything we can to equip you with the tools and resources you need to grow your own practice so you can help as many businesses as possible. It’s also worth pointing out that just one new client should easily cover the license fee required to participate in this program, so for active partners, achieving a positive ROI should be the minimum expected goal. But again…no guarantees. This is still your business, and it’s up to you to run and grow.
