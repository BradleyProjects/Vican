{WHAT YOU'LL LEARN}

DIGITAL MARKETING
IS CONSTANTLY CHANGING

Every year, thousands of the world’s smartest marketers come together to learn what’s new, what’s hot, and what’s actually working RIGHT NOW in digital marketing. From the latest marketing tools and trends, AI and efficiency tactics, employee acquisition and retention, to scaling while saving time and money, you’ll learn this and more at this year’s Traffic & Conversion Summit…

NEW TRAFFIC
CHANNELS

With changing customer behavior, it’s harder than ever before to advertise on traffic channels like Meta. T&C 2024 will break down the traffic sources you should be investing in from influencer marketing to TikTok, to AI and audio. T&C will tell you how you can leverage them ALL to get more attention while lowering acquisition costs.

Customer-Centric Marketing

In reality a successful business comes down to one thing… your customers. And at T&C 2024 you’ll learn to get to know your customer inside and out through avatar profiling, acquisition and retention tactics and efficiencies from AI CX to onboarding and referral strategies and so much more… BUT most importantly how to provide your customers with the value and content they need to generate the sales you want through the customer value journey.

NEW CONVERSION
BREAKTHROUGH

Our digital marketing experts have done thousands of landing page, order form, ad, and email testing in the last 6 months and they’re ready to share dozens of “copy and paste” split-tests that you can  roll out at your own company. Not only that… but they’ll show you how to optimize like a champ so that you can take your business to the next level. 

PLUS ULTRA-CURRENT BREAKOUT SESSIONS

T&C 2024 will have 8 different topic tracks jam-packed with actionable content. Hear from marketing’s best about what it means to thrive in a digital-first world. We’ll be covering AI, what tools to use in 2023 and beyond, how to win big on social, content marketing funnels (that actually work!), the creator economy, list building and segmentation, what’s working on email, and much, much more.
