Want to boost your sales? Partner with an experienced advertising agency.

Search engine marketing (SEM), also known as google ads, can be a highly effective way for advertising agencies and advertising agencies in Johannesburg to drive traffic and increase sales for their clients. By using targeted advertising on search engines like Google and Bing, advertising agencies can help their clients reach potential customers at the moment they are searching for products or services like those offered by the business.


This can lead to higher conversion rates and a better return on investment (ROI) than other forms of advertising. However, the effectiveness of SEM will depend on factors such as the industry, target audience, ad campaign, and competition. It is recommended for advertising agencies to have a detailed and well-planned strategy, as well as continuous monitoring and optimization to maximize results for their clients.

Our Advertising Channels We Offer You

Google or Bing Search Ads

Google Search Ads is a pay-per-click (PPC) advertising program offered by Google. We create and place ads that appear at the top of Google search results pages when customers search for keywords related to the business's products or services.


Google Search Ads offers a wide range of targeting options, including geographic location, demographics, interests, and keywords, allowing businesses to reach specific audiences that are most likely to be interested in their products or services. This feature makes it an effective way to increase visibility, drive traffic, and generate leads.

Google Shopping Ads

Google Shopping ads allows businesses to display products from their inventory in the search engine results pages (SERPs) when users search for related keywords. Within these ads we include an image of the product, its price, and other details such as the name of the business and a link to the product page on the business's website.


These ads can be highly effective in driving sales and increasing visibility for businesses that sell physical goods online. They are especially useful for product-based businesses. They are more likely to attract clicks and generate sales.

Display Ads

Google Display Ads are a popular form of online advertising used by businesses and advertising agencies in Johannesburg to promote their products or services through various ad formats such as banner ads, images, text, and videos on websites, social media platforms, and mobile apps. These ads are placed on third-party websites, which are part of the Google Display Network (GDN) to reach a wide audience.


One of the key advantages of Google Display Ads for advertising agencies in Johannesburg is remarketing, which allows businesses to target users who have previously interacted with their website or mobile app by displaying ads to them as they browse other sites on the GDN. Targeting specific demographics such as age, gender, location, and interests based on the data that Google collects from users’ browsing history allows businesses to reach a highly targeted audience and increase the chances of conversions. Advertising agencies in Johannesburg can use Google Display Ads to effectively reach their desired audience and achieve their advertising goals.

Performance Max Ads

We use Performance Max to help clients businesses achieve their performance objectives, such as driving conversions, increasing brand awareness, and reaching specific audiences.


It offers advanced targeting options and real-time performance optimisation capabilities to deliver highly relevant and personalised ad experiences to users, across all devices and platforms.

Remarking Ads

Our Remarketing also know as retargeting allows our clients businesses to reach users who have previously interacted with their website or mobile app. Remarketing ads can be highly effective in driving conversions and increasing sales, as they are delivered to users who have already shown an interest in the business's products or services.


Additionally, Remarketing ads can be used to re-engage with users who abandoned a shopping cart, or to promote upsell or cross-sell offers.

Social Media Ads

We use social media advertising can be highly effective in increasing brand awareness, driving website traffic, and generating leads and sales. It can also be a cost-effective way to reach a large and engaged audience, depending on the platform and targeting options you choose.


Social media ads come in a variety of formats, depending on the platform being used, but the most common types include sponsored posts, sponsored stories, and display ads. The main platforms for social media advertising include Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok and many more.

Advertising Management Process

#1 - Setting Advertising Goals: The first step in the advertising management process is to set clear and measurable goals for your Advertising Agency. This could include increasing website traffic, driving sales, creating leads or raising brand awareness.

#2 - Keyword Research: Once your advertising goals are set, the next step is to conduct keyword research to identify the best keywords to target in your campaign. This will help you reach the right audience and improve the performance of your ads.

#3 - Ad Creation: After researching keywords, we will create ads that are tailored to your target audience and optimised for performance. This will include writing ad copy, selecting images or videos, and selecting the right ad format, target audience's and detailed targeting.

#4 - Campaign Management: Once your ads are created, we will manage your campaign to ensure it is performing well. This will include monitoring your ad spend, analysing performance data, and making adjustments to your campaign to improve performance.

#5 - Measurement and Optimisation: The final step in the advertising management process is to measure the performance of your campaign and make adjustments as needed to optimise performance. This will include testing different ad formats, researching new keywords, analysing and improving search terms targeting different audiences, and adjusting your ad spend to maximise ROI.
