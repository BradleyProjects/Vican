A.I. Website

Webshure • Jan 15, 2023

The concept behind AI Websites is straightforward: it’s all about creating a website experience that directly relates to a visitor’s unique situation. This is accomplished by changing the messaging and content of a site based on things like time of day, the number of previous visits to the site, visitor’s physical location and more. 

﻿

A.I. works automatically which leaves you with more time to focus on your business, or maybe even take more time for yourself, imagine that! 

View some of the A.I. actions below!

  • Time & Date

    Create a trigger once and use it whenever! Set up your triggers to recur at any time, and on any date you want!

  • Number of Visits

    A first-time visitor is most likely looking for something different than a returning customer. Easily determine what visitors see based on how often they have previously engaged with your site.

  • Notification Bars

    Run a message across the top of your website to highlight a promotion, or deliver a special message to your visitors.

  • Notification Bars

    Craft specialised calls-to-actions, messages and layouts that best match the device your visitor is using. Use geo-targeting to ensure your site visitors are seeing content that is relevant to their physical location.

  • Campaign Marketing

    Running an ad campaign? Create a unique link with the Campaign URL trigger. Once clicked, this link will take your visitor to your site, but show them an experience that directly relates to your ad.

  • Seasonal Personalisation

    Personalise your website at different seasons and events such as Christmas, Halloween, Valentines, Easter and so much more!
